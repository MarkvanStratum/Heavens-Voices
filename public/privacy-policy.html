Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 2023

At Heaven's Voices, we hold the sanctity of your private reflections in the highest regard. This Privacy Policy outlines how BESMIT LIMITED (Hong Kong) collects, protects, and handles your information.

1. Information We Collect

In order to provide a personalized spiritual journey, we collect minimal but necessary data:

  • Account Data: Your email address and password for account creation and authentication.
  • Dialogue History: We store your chat history with the biblical figures to allow you to resume your conversations across different sessions.
  • Technical Data: Standard server logs, including IP addresses and browser types, to ensure the security and stability of our sanctuary.

2. Use of Information

Your data is used solely to:

  • Provide and maintain the AI chat service.
  • Secure your account against unauthorized access.
  • Improve the responses and theological depth of our AI models.
  • Communicate essential service updates or offering details.

3. Confidentiality of Dialogues

We believe your spiritual inquiries are private. We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal chat logs to third-party advertisers. Your conversations are processed through secure API connections to our AI providers, where they are used only for generating responses and are protected by enterprise-grade encryption.

4. Data Retention

We retain your chat history for as long as your account remains active. You may request the deletion of your account and all associated dialogues at any time by contacting our support team.

5. Cookies and Tracking

We use essential cookies to keep you signed in to the sanctuary. These are small files stored on your device that allow our system to recognize your browser and session. We do not use intrusive tracking cookies for advertising purposes.

6. Secure Offerings

Payment information for our sacred offerings is handled by third-party payment processors. BESMIT LIMITED does not store your full credit card details on our own servers.

7. International Transfers

As a global service operated by BESMIT LIMITED in Hong Kong, your data may be processed on servers located outside of your home country. By using the sanctuary, you consent to this transfer of information.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to access the data we hold about you, request corrections, or ask for total data erasure. To exercise these rights, please reach out to us through the provided contact channels.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding your privacy or this policy, please email us or visit the help section of the sanctuary.
